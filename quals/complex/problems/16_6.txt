<problem><p>Let \(f:D\to\mathbb{C}\) be a nonconstant holomorphic function on the unit disc. Assume \(f(0)=1\) and \(Re(f(z))\geq0\). Prove that for all \(|z|\leq1\),</p><p>\[|f(z)|\leq\frac{1+|z|}{1-|z|}\]</p></problem><solution><p>Because \(Re(f)\geq0\) for all \(z\in D\), we know that \(f\) maps into the right half plane. Take a Mobius transformation which sends \(1\) to \(0\) and sends the right half plane to the unit disc. \(m(z)=\frac{z-1}{z+1}\) works. Then look at</p><p>\[m\circ f:D\to D\]</p><p>By construction, \(m(f(0))=m(1)=0\). Thus the Schwarz lemma gives that for all \(z\in D\), </p><p>\[|m(f(z))|\leq|z|\]</p><p> </p><p>\[\left|\frac{f(z)-1}{f(z)+1}\right|\leq|z|\]</p><p> </p><p>\[|f(z)-1|\leq|zf(z)+z|\]</p><p> </p><p>\[|f(z)|-1\leq|f(z)-1|\leq|zf(z)+z|\leq|zf(z)|+|z|\]</p><p> </p><p>\[|f(z)|-|zf(z)|\leq1+|z|\]</p><p> </p><p>\[|f(z)|(1-|z|)\leq1+|z|\]</p><p> </p><p>\[|f(z)|\leq\frac{1+|z|}{1-|z|}\]</p></solution><topics><MobiusTransformation/><FunctionBounds/><Schwarz/></topics><instructors><a/><c/></instructors>
